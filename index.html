Soul is a 2020 American computer-animated film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures. ... The story follows a middle school music teacher named Joe Gardner, who seeks to reunite his soul and his body after they are accidentally separated, just before his big break as a jazz musician.
